About The Access Agency

  • Back

We exist to make visitor experiences fulfilling and equitable and for everyone. Our aim is to be a world-class partner in access and information for the visitor economy.

We’re qualified Access Consultants, award-winning creatives, and we live with disability.

We’re qualified Access Consultants, award-winning creatives, and we live with disability.

People liked what we were doing – so we made more. We reviewed our favourite places in our hometown of Melbourne and created a directory. Then we we’re approached by John Kenwright at Parks Victoria to create a video series of 10 parks.

In 2021, The Access Agency was born. We saw the growing need to help destinations connect and provide for visitors with access needs and we haven’t looked back since.
