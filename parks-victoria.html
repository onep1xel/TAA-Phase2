Parks in Victoria are for everyone.

They’re beautiful, regenerative and should be easy to visit.

But some aren’t especially easy to get around if you have a reduced mobility.

Challenge

Parks Victoria wanted to showcase the ‘all-abilities access’ of 10 selected parks. Scattered around the state and not all strictly ‘accessible’, each have their own character and appeal.

Process

We consulted with PV staff and with members of the disability community to write, shoot and produce the videos in a way that showcased access while providing the genuine excitement in visiting these beautiful places.

Result

The first video was released on ‘International Wheelchair Day’ in March 2021 and within three days had reached 2,000+ people.

Three months later it had reached 10K views. Moreover, the first video was shared widely between friends, family and people with reduced mobility.

Those who benefit the most are getting the information they need to plan and visit with confidence.
